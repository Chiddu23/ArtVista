Our Recent Favourites

Irises (May 1890)

The evening before van Gogh checked himself out of the Saint-Rémy psychiatric hospital, where he stayed for 374 days from 8 May 1889 to 16 May 1890, he painted four still life works of flowers.

Storm on the Sea of Galilee

Storm on the Sea of Galilee was a piece of art painted in 1633, by the Dutch Golden Age painter Rembrandt van Rijn.

Dogs Playing Poker

"Dogs Playing Poker" is a famous series of paintings created by Cassius Marcellus Coolidge in the early 1900s. The artwork portrays a group of anthropomorphic dogs engaging in a friendly game of poker, showcasing their humorous and relatable expressions.
